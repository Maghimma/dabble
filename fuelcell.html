⚡ Hydrogen Fuel Cell
2H₂ + O₂ → 2H₂O  +  electricity
Negative electrode (anode) — H₂ side
2H₂ → 4H⁺ + 4e⁻
Positive electrode (cathode) — O₂ side
O₂ + 4H⁺ + 4e⁻ → 2H₂O
Hydrogen is oxidised, releasing electrons that flow round the circuit (powering the bulb). The H⁺ ions cross the membrane. The only product is water — clean energy (used in spacecraft & cars).
